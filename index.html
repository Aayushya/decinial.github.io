​School of Law, Christ University (SLCU) has a vibrant and active mooting culture, with a student population of roughly 1200 students. The Decinial, 2017(MCS) of School of Law, Christ University is a student run committee under the guidance of faculty co-coordinators, which seeks to channelize the students on the mooting path. The MCS is a team of dedicated students who continuously work towards achieving new heights in the mooting arena. 

The primary goal of the Decinial, 2017is to provide all SLCU students the opportunities to harbour research, oral advocacy and legal drafting skills and other essential prowess to mould an individual into an adept mooter. In order to achieve this objective, the Decinial, 2017organizes various events like Mooting Primer for all the novice mooters at SLCU, Intro-Moot for the first years, IMC (Internal Mooting Championship) wherein all the first and second year students compete with one another to victory, Internal National Rounds, Internal International National Round, Internal Jessup Qualifying Rounds and Inter-College National Moot Court Competition

The Decinial, 2017doesn’t just limit itself to Intra-College activities it also indulges itself in various Inter-College competitions. SLCU’s students have taken part in many various Moot Court Competitions hosted by many law colleges. Teams of SLCU have a long tradition of excellence in appellate advocacy and their talent has succeeded in winning many Moot Court Competitions in India and across the borders.
